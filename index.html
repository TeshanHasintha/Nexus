Server Rules

Community Guidelines

Our rules ensure a fair, fun, and immersive experience for all players. Please read them carefully.

Roleplay Standards

  • Always stay in character (IC) at all times. Use /ooc or designated OOC channels for out-of-character communication.
  • No powergaming — you cannot force actions on other players without giving them a chance to respond.
  • No metagaming — using information obtained OOC while IC is strictly prohibited.
  • Value your character's life (NVL). Fear for your life in dangerous situations.
  • No random deathmatch (RDM) or vehicle deathmatch (VDM). All violence must have proper roleplay reasoning.
  • Combat logging — disconnecting to avoid consequences is a bannable offense.

Chat & Communication

  • No hate speech, harassment, or discriminatory language of any kind.
  • No spamming chat channels or voice communications.
  • English is the primary language for all server communications.
  • No advertising other servers or communities without staff approval.
  • Keep OOC discussions out of IC channels and vice versa.

Gameplay Conduct

  • No exploiting bugs or glitches. Report them to staff immediately.
  • No using third-party cheats, mods, or scripts that give unfair advantages.
  • No stream sniping or using streams to gain IC information.
  • Follow staff instructions at all times. Arguing with staff in-game is not permitted.
  • No disrupting server events or organized roleplay scenarios.

Prohibited Content

  • No explicit sexual content or ERP (Erotic Roleplay) in public areas.
  • No graphic violence descriptions beyond what the game naturally depicts.
  • No references to real-world tragic events or sensitive topics for shock value.
  • No doxxing or sharing personal information of other players.
